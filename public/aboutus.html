About us

Karma is a social media platform that provides people with volunteering opportunities and a chance to connect with like-minded individuals who want to make a change in their community for the better.

The principle of Karma dictates that all the good that you do will eventually be returned to you. We want to create a community of people who want to contribute to their communities.
